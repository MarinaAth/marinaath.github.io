I am a postdoctoral researcher in bioinformatics with the Translational Microbiome Science group at Universitaetsklinikum Tübingen. Over the course of my graduate and postdoctoral research, I have handled a wide range of data — genomic, transcriptomic, metagenomic and clinical — across multiple organisms. My research interests are evolving toward machine learning applications in cancer research, particularly in developing effective data representations for complex biological datasets — returning to approaches I explored during my MSc work on genomic sequence representations.
